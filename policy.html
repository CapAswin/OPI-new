Introduction

Your trust is our priority. At Opulent Prime, we understand that maintaining the confidentiality and security of your personal and financial information is critical to our partnership. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information within our ecosystem.

Who we are

Opulent Prime Investment DWC LLC ("OPI", "we", "us", or "our") is an institutional asset management firm. Our digital presence serves as a secure gateway for professional investors to access bespoke opportunities and reporting.

How we use information

  • Service delivery: onboarding, portfolio management, and transaction processing.
  • Structuring opportunities: tailoring mandates to risk and liquidity requirements.
  • Communication: performance reporting, insights, and regulatory updates.
  • Legal requirements: compliance with applicable AML and related regulations.
  • Platform enhancement: improving UX, security, and operational resilience.

Information sharing

We maintain a strict policy of confidentiality. We do not sell your personal data. However, to fulfill our obligations, information may be shared with licensed partners, custodians, and regulatory bodies as required.
